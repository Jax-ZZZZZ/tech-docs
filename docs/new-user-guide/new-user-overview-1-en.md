#### Before Using POS& Credit Card Machine

Before you start using the POS system, please carefully verify that the following information is accurate:

- Restaurant Name
- Business Address
- Business Phone Number
- Sales Tax Rate
- Bank Deposit Account

⚠️ **Important Notice:**  
The information above directly affects the following:

- Receipt and invoice display
- Daily and monthly reports
- Transaction settlement and deposits
- Tax compliance
- Ongoing technical support and customer service

Please make sure all information is confirmed **before the POS system goes live**  
to avoid settlement delays, reporting errors, or additional adjustments later.
