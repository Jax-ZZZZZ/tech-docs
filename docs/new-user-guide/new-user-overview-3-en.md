#### Credit Card Surcharge & Compliance

Visa conducts regular compliance reviews.  
If any of the following violations are identified, the restaurant may face **significant fines or account risk**:

- Non-compliant credit card surcharges
- Improper minimum purchase amount settings
- Incorrect cash discount or pricing display practices

---

#### Key Compliance Guidelines

#### Minimum Purchase Amount
- **Debit Cards: Minimum purchase amounts are NOT allowed**
- **Credit Cards: Minimum purchase amount may not exceed $10**

---

#### Cash Discount
- Menus and pricing must display the **credit card price**
- Customers may receive a discount when paying with cash
- ‚ùå Displaying a cash price and adding an extra charge for credit cards  
  is considered a surcharge and is **not allowed**

---

#### Credit Card Surcharge
- Surcharges do **not** apply to debit cards
- May be charged as a flat amount or a percentage
- **Maximum surcharge may not exceed 3% of the transaction amount**
- Clear signage must be posted at the restaurant entrance and point of sale

---

#### States Where Credit Card Surcharges Are Prohibited
- Massachusetts (MA)
- Connecticut (CT)
- Maine (ME)
- Oklahoma (OK)

---

#### Other Fees
- **Convenience Fee**  
  - Only permitted for payments made through third-party platforms  
    (such as BeyondMenu Online, Uber Eats, DoorDash)
- **Service Fee**  
  - Only allowed for government agencies, hospitals, schools, and other public institutions
