#### Debit Card Pre-Authorization Notice

When a **debit card with a low available balance (typically under $1,000)** is used,  
restaurant transactions may trigger a **pre-authorization hold** from the bank:

- The bank may temporarily hold an amount **approximately 20% higher than the original transaction**
- This additional amount is intended to cover potential tips
- The hold helps prevent the final settlement amount from exceeding the available balance
