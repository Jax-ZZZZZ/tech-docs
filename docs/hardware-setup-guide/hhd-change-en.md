### HDD Tray Disassembly

<img
  src="/images/hhdtraydisassembly.png"
  alt="hhd tray disassembly"
  class="img-md"
/>

**Step 1:**  
Pull the HDD tray handle and lift it up.

**Step 2:**  
When reinstalling the HDD tray, please make sure the tray is inserted in the **correct direction**.

---

### HDD & SSD Disassembly

<img
  src="/images/hhddisassembly.png"
  alt="hhd disassembly"
  class="img-md"
/>

**Step 1:**  
Carefully open the four tray hinges in opposite directions, as shown in the image.

**Step 2:**  
Align and assemble the HDD (or SSD) bottom plate with the tray bottom.
