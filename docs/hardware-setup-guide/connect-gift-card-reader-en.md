#### MSR (Gift Card Reader) Installation Guide
<img
  src="/images/msr.png"
  alt="MSR bracket"
  class="img-md"
/>
**Step 1:**  
Use screws to secure the MSR bracket to the back side of the monitor

**Step 2:**  
Insert the MSR module into the MSR bracket

**Step 3:**  
Connect the cable to the MSR port

**Step 4:**  
Contact Beyond Menu Technical Support to configure and test the MSR in the POS system
