You should find:

#### 2. Remove the Back Panel

<img
  src="/images/computerback.png"
  alt="computer back"
  class="img-md"
/>

On the back of the computer, there are **Push Sign** labels on both sides.  
Press and slide to remove the back panel.

---

#### 3. Connect the Power

<img
  src="/images/powerconnecting.png"
  alt="power connecting"
  class="img-md"
/>

- Locate the **round black power port** in the center
- Connect the power adapter and plug it into a wall outlet
- The **green light** on the adapter indicates power is connected

---

#### 4. Connect the Network

<img
  src="/images/io.png"
  alt="i/o ports"
  class="img-md"
/>


- The **LAN (network) port** is next to the power port, 5 LAN as in pciture
- Connect an Ethernet cable to a **router or network switch**

- Physical power button  
- Located **below the indicator light on the right side of the screen**
- Press to turn on the computer
