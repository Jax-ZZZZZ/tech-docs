#### PAX A80 Device Connection Guide
<br />
<img
  src="/images/s80.png"
  alt="pax s80"
  class="img-md"
/>
<br />

**Step 1:**  
Open the package and confirm the following items are included:  
- PAX A80 payment terminal  
- Power adapter  
- Power cable  

**Step 2:**  
Use an Ethernet cable to connect the **LAN port** on the back of the terminal  
to a router or network switch.

**Step 3:**  
Connect the power cable and turn on the device.  
Once powered on, please contact **Beyond Menu Technical Support**  
for system configuration and testing.
