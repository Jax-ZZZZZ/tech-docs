## PAX A80 Basic Operations Guide
> This guide also applies to  PAX A920 Pro, as the operation procedures are the same.

### 1. Sale (Payment)

Used for normal card or electronic payment transactions.

**Steps:**

1. On the home screen, tap **Sale**
2. Enter the transaction amount
3. Select a payment method:
   - Tap / Apple Pay (Contactless)
   - Chip (Insert EMV card)
   - Swipe (Magnetic stripe)
4. Follow the on-screen instructions
5. Customer signs if required
6. Print or send the receipt after the transaction is approved

### 3. Add Tips

Used to add tips to a transaction that is **already completed but not yet batched**.

**Steps:**

1. On the home screen, tap **Func**
2. Select **Tip Menu**
3. Select the corresponding transaction
4. Enter the tip amount
5. Confirm submission
6. The tip will be settled together during Batch

> Commonly used when tips are added after the customer signs the receipt.

### 4. Batch (Settlement / Close Batch)

Used to **submit all completed transactions of the day to the bank for settlement**.

**Steps:**

1. On the home screen, tap **Func**
2. Select **Batch**
3. Confirm **Batch to Close**
4. Wait for the system to complete processing
5. Print the Batch report (recommended to keep for records)

> âš ï¸ It is strongly recommended to run Batch **at least once at the end of each business day**.

### 5. Force Auth (Force Authorization / Force Tip)

Used when a **bank authorization code (Auth Code)** has already been obtained,  
commonly in cases of network issues or special tip adjustments.

**Steps:**

1. On the home screen, tap **Force Auth**
2. Enter the transaction amount or tip amount
3. Follow the prompts to enter the card number and expiration date
4. Enter the **Auth Code** shown on the receipt
5. Confirm to complete the transaction

> âš ï¸ This function carries higher risk and should only be used when there is a clear possibility of customer disputes.
