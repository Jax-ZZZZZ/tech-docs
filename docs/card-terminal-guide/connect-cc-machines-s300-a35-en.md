#### PAX A35 Device Connection Guide
<br />
<img
  src="/images/a35en.png"
  alt="pax a35"
  class="img-md"
/>
<br />

**Step 1:**  
Open the package and confirm the following items are included:  
- PAX A35 payment terminal  
- Type-C power cable and power adapter  
- Connection cable (hub cable)

**Step 2:**  
Use a Phillips screwdriver to open the back cover of the terminal.  
Connect the **thick Type-C cable** to the **power port** on the back of the device,  
then close the cover and tighten the screws.

**Step 3:**  
Use an Ethernet cable to connect to the **red LAN port** on the connection cable,  
then connect the other end to a router or network switch.

**Step 4:**  
Plug the **Type-C connector** from the power cable into the **Type-C port** on the connection cable.

**Step 5:**  
After all connections are completed, the device will power on automatically.  
Once powered on, please contact **Beyond Menu Technical Support**  
for system configuration and testing.

#### PAX S300 Device Connection Guide
<br />
<img
  src="/images/S300en.png"
  alt="pax s300"
  class="img-md"
/>
<br />

**Step 1:**  
Open the package and confirm the following items are included:  
- PAX S300 payment terminal  
- Power cable  
- Connection cable (hub cable)

**Step 2:**  
Use an Ethernet cable to connect to the **red LAN port** on the connection cable,  
then connect the other end to a router or network switch.  
**The blue and yellow ports are not used and should not be connected.**

**Step 3:**  
After connecting the power cable, the device will power on automatically.  
Once the device is powered on, please contact **Beyond Menu Technical Support**  
for system configuration and testing.
